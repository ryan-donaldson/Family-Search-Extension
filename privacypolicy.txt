# Privacy Policy for Family Search Assistant

## Introduction
This privacy policy document outlines the types of personal information received and collected by Family Search Assistant and how it is used.

## Information Collection and Use
Family Search Assistant may collect the following information from its users:

- **User Activity**: Only accessed to allow inputs to be saved when extension is closed and is not collected or transmitted anywhere else.

Please note that Family Search Assistant does not collect more information than is necessary to fulfill its stated purposes.

## Data Storage
The data collected by Family Search Assistant is stored:

- Locally on the user's device: Text inputs and mouse clicks to allow users to easier track those they're indexing.

## Data Sharing
Family Search Assistant does not share, sell, rent, or trade personally identifiable information with third parties for their commercial purposes.

## User Control and Access
Users can access and control their data by:

- Viewing their data within the extension's text boxes.
- Deleting their data by uninstalling the extension or simply deleting the text in the boxes.

## Changes to This Privacy Policy
Ryan Donaldson, extension developer, reserves the right to make changes to this privacy policy at any time. Users will be notified of any changes through extension updates.
